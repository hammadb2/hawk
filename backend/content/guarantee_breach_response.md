## PART I — RECITALS AND DEFINITIONS

1.1 Recitals

This Breach Response Guarantee (the "Guarantee") is issued by HAWK Security, a division of AKB Company Ltd ("HAWK", "we", "us", or "our") to the subscribing entity ("Subscriber", "you", or "your") upon execution of a valid HAWK Shield Subscription Agreement and payment of all applicable subscription fees.

This Guarantee is not an insurance policy. HAWK is not a licensed insurer and this Guarantee does not constitute insurance under the Insurance Act (Alberta) or any equivalent legislation in any other jurisdiction.

This Guarantee is expressly limited in scope, duration, eligibility, and remedy. The Subscriber acknowledges having read, understood, and accepted all terms herein as a condition of receiving this Guarantee.

1.2 Definitions

For the purposes of this Guarantee the following definitions shall apply:

Active Subscription

A HAWK Shield subscription for which all fees are current, no payment is overdue by more than three (3) calendar days, and which has not been cancelled, suspended, or terminated.

Attack Vector

The specific technical pathway through which an unauthorized party gained or attempted to gain access to the Subscriber's systems or data.

Breach

A confirmed, verified, unauthorized access to the Subscriber's systems or data by an external party through an Attack Vector within HAWK's Monitored Scope, as determined solely by HAWK following investigation.

Business Day

Any day that is not a Saturday, Sunday, or statutory holiday in the Province of Alberta, Canada.

Claim Window

The period of twenty-four (24) hours commencing from the moment the Subscriber first becomes aware, or reasonably ought to have become aware, of a potential Breach.

Compliance Period

The continuous ninety (90) day period immediately preceding the date of a reported Incident during which the Subscriber must have maintained full compliance with all obligations in Part IV.

Coverage Amount

The maximum reimbursement available under this Guarantee, expressed as a credit toward future HAWK Shield subscription fees, as specified in Part III.

Critical Finding

Any security vulnerability classified as Critical severity, defined as a CVSS base score of 9.0 or higher, or any finding that HAWK's AI interpretation engine classifies as actively exploitable at the time of detection.

HAWK Approved Forensic Investigator

An independent cybersecurity forensic firm pre-approved in writing by HAWK prior to commencement of any investigation. No forensic costs incurred without prior written HAWK approval shall be eligible for reimbursement.

HAWK Monitored Scope

Exclusively: the primary domain registered in the Subscriber's HAWK account, publicly accessible subdomains, external email security configurations (SPF, DKIM, DMARC), SSL/TLS configuration, publicly accessible network ports, and credentials appearing in public breach databases monitored by HAWK.

HAWK Readiness Score

The numerical security posture score between 0 and 100 calculated daily by HAWK's automated systems based on outstanding Findings and compliance with remediation timelines.

High Finding

Any security vulnerability classified as High severity, defined as a CVSS base score of 7.0 to 8.9.

Incident Response Costs

Documented, verified, reasonable fees paid to a HAWK Approved Forensic Investigator for forensic investigation services only. Expressly excludes: legal fees, regulatory fines, notification costs, credit monitoring, PR fees, business interruption losses, ransom payments, hardware replacement, data recovery, reputational damages, and any other costs not specifically listed as covered in Part III.

Material Technology Change

Any modification to the Subscriber's domain registration, DNS configuration, web hosting provider, email hosting provider, SSL certificate provider, CMS, e-commerce platform, booking software, payment processor, cloud storage provider, or any other technology component that HAWK monitors.

Monitored Domain

The single primary domain registered in the Subscriber's HAWK account. Additional domains are not included unless the Subscriber has purchased a multi-domain HAWK Enterprise subscription.

Qualifying Breach

A Breach that meets every condition set forth in Part II, Part IV, and Part V of this Guarantee, as determined solely and finally by HAWK following investigation.

Readiness Score Threshold

A HAWK Readiness Score of eighty-five (85) or higher out of one hundred (100).

Remediation

The complete resolution of a Finding as verified exclusively by HAWK's automated scanning and verification systems. Client self-reporting does not constitute Remediation.

Reimbursement

The Coverage Amount applied as a credit to the Subscriber's HAWK Shield subscription account. Not payable in cash, bank transfer, cheque, cryptocurrency, or any other form.


## PART II — GUARANTEE COVERAGE

2.1 What This Guarantee Covers

Subject to all terms, conditions, exclusions, limitations, and obligations set forth in this Guarantee, HAWK will provide Reimbursement toward verified Incident Response Costs incurred by the Subscriber in connection with a Qualifying Breach.

2.2 Coverage Is Strictly Limited

This Guarantee covers only and exclusively the following:

Verified Incident Response Costs paid to a HAWK Approved Forensic Investigator, up to the Coverage Amount specified in Part III.

Only Incidents that arise directly and exclusively from an External Attack Vector within HAWK's Monitored Scope.

Only Incidents that occur during an Active Subscription period that has been continuous for a minimum of ninety (90) days from the Subscription Commencement Date.

2.3 What This Guarantee Does Not Cover

This Guarantee expressly does not cover, and HAWK shall have no obligation whatsoever with respect to, any of the following:

Any costs, losses, damages, fines, penalties, or expenses of any nature other than verified Incident Response Costs paid to a HAWK Approved Forensic Investigator.

Any Incident that originates in whole or in part from human error, negligence, or action by any employee, officer, director, contractor, agent, or representative of the Subscriber.

Any Incident involving social engineering, phishing, spear phishing, whaling, vishing, smishing, business email compromise, or any form of deception or manipulation targeting any person.

Any Incident involving insider threats, whether malicious, negligent, or accidental.

Any Incident involving any Device used by the Subscriber or its employees, contractors, or agents.

Any Incident involving any third-party software, application, plugin, extension, theme, library, or dependency.

Any Incident involving any SaaS platform including but not limited to Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, Dropbox, Salesforce, HubSpot, QuickBooks, Xero, Jane App, Dentrix, Clio, or any other cloud-based service.

Any Incident involving payment processing, point of sale, electronic health records, or practice management software.

Any Incident involving ransomware or malware installed through any means other than a directly exploited vulnerability in a publicly accessible service on the Monitored Domain that HAWK had flagged and the Subscriber had failed to remediate within the required timeline.

Any Incident occurring within the first ninety (90) days of the Subscription Commencement Date.

Any Incident occurring during any period when the Subscriber's HAWK Readiness Score was below the Readiness Score Threshold.

Any Incident involving systems, domains, subdomains, or services not included in HAWK's Monitored Scope.

Any Incident where the Subscriber has made, or is eligible to make, a claim under any cyber liability insurance policy for the same event.

Any Incident involving a pre-existing compromise that commenced prior to the Subscription Commencement Date.

Any Incident where the Subscriber failed to notify HAWK within the Claim Window.

Any Incident where a Material Technology Change was made without providing HAWK with written notice at least forty-eight (48) hours prior.

Any consequential, indirect, punitive, exemplary, special, or reputational damages, business interruption losses, regulatory fines, legal fees, notification costs, or ransom payments of any nature.


## PART III — COVERAGE AMOUNT AND REIMBURSEMENT

3.1 Coverage Amount

The maximum Coverage Amount available under this Guarantee is two thousand five hundred Canadian dollars ($2,500 CAD) per Incident, applied exclusively as a credit toward future HAWK Shield subscription fees.

3.2 Reimbursement Format

All Reimbursement shall be provided exclusively as a credit applied to the Subscriber's HAWK Shield subscription account. Reimbursement is not payable in any other form including cash, cheque, bank transfer, electronic funds transfer, cryptocurrency, gift card, or any other monetary instrument.

3.3 Claim Frequency

Only one (1) Claim may be submitted and approved per rolling twelve (12) month period. Submission of a Claim does not guarantee approval.

3.4 Coverage Adjustment

HAWK reserves the right to adjust the Coverage Amount at any time with thirty (30) days written notice to the Subscriber. Coverage Amounts applicable at the time of a Claim submission shall govern that Claim regardless of subsequent adjustments.

3.5 No Assignment

The Subscriber's rights under this Guarantee are personal and may not be assigned, transferred, pledged, or otherwise disposed of to any third party.

3.6 Subrogation

In the event HAWK makes any Reimbursement under this Guarantee, HAWK shall be subrogated to all rights and remedies of the Subscriber against any third party responsible for or contributing to the Incident, up to the amount of Reimbursement provided.


## PART IV — SUBSCRIBER OBLIGATIONS

4.1 Continuous Compliance Required

The Guarantee is void unless the Subscriber has maintained continuous and uninterrupted compliance with every obligation in this Part IV throughout the entire Compliance Period.

4.2 Remediation Obligations

All Critical Findings must be fully Remediated within twenty-four (24) hours of first Notification. Where technical implementation is required by a third party, the deadline is forty-eight (48) hours. No extensions are available.

All High Findings must be fully Remediated within forty-eight (48) hours of first Notification. No extensions are available.

All Medium Findings must be fully Remediated within fourteen (14) calendar days of first Notification.

All Low Findings must be fully Remediated within sixty (60) calendar days of first Notification.

Remediation is exclusively verified by HAWK's automated systems. The Subscriber must actively trigger verification through the HAWK portal's Verify Fix function.

Any Finding that is dismissed, marked as accepted risk, snoozed, or otherwise not actively remediated shall be treated as unresolved for the purposes of this Guarantee.

4.3 Readiness Score Obligation

The Subscriber must maintain a HAWK Readiness Score at or above the Readiness Score Threshold of eighty-five (85) out of one hundred (100) continuously throughout the Compliance Period. A single day below the Readiness Score Threshold voids the Guarantee until the score has been restored above the threshold for a continuous period of thirty (30) days.

4.4 Subscription Obligation

The Subscriber must maintain an Active Subscription continuously throughout the Compliance Period. Any lapse in payment, suspension, or cancellation and reactivation resets the Compliance Period and the ninety (90) day minimum subscription requirement.

4.5 Onboarding Obligation

The Subscriber must complete HAWK's onboarding process, including participation in the initial onboarding call, within fourteen (14) calendar days of the Subscription Commencement Date. Failure to complete onboarding within this period voids the Guarantee permanently for that subscription term.

4.6 Technology Change Notification Obligation

The Subscriber must provide HAWK with written notification via email to support@securedbyhawk.com at least forty-eight (48) hours prior to any Material Technology Change. Failure to provide advance written notification of any Material Technology Change voids the Guarantee for any Incident occurring after such change.

4.7 Alert Acknowledgment Obligation

The Subscriber must have enabled and maintained all HAWK notification channels throughout the Compliance Period and must acknowledge receipt of all Critical Finding Notifications within four (4) hours of delivery. Failure to acknowledge a Critical Finding Notification within four (4) hours shall be treated as non-compliance with the remediation timeline for that Finding.

4.8 Third Party Access Obligation

The Subscriber must not grant any third party administrative or unrestricted access to any system within the Monitored Scope without providing HAWK with written notification within twenty-four (24) hours of granting such access.

4.9 Cooperation Obligation

The Subscriber must cooperate fully with HAWK and any HAWK Approved Forensic Investigator during any Claim investigation. Failure to provide requested documentation, access, or cooperation within the timeframes specified by HAWK voids the Claim.


## PART V — ELIGIBILITY CONDITIONS

5.1 All Conditions Must Be Met

A Claim is eligible for consideration only if every single one of the following conditions is met simultaneously. Failure to meet any single condition voids the entire Claim regardless of the merits of any other condition.

The Incident constitutes a Qualifying Breach as defined in this Guarantee.

The Subscriber has maintained an Active Subscription continuously for a minimum of ninety (90) days immediately preceding the Incident date.

The Subscriber's HAWK Readiness Score was at or above the Readiness Score Threshold continuously throughout the entire Compliance Period.

No Critical Finding was unresolved for more than twenty-four (24) hours at any point during the Compliance Period (forty-eight (48) hours where technical implementation was required).

No High Finding was unresolved for more than forty-eight (48) hours at any point during the Compliance Period.

No Finding was dismissed, marked as accepted risk, or otherwise not actively remediated at any point during the Compliance Period.

No Material Technology Change was made during the Compliance Period without forty-eight (48) hours prior written notice to HAWK.

The Subscriber completed HAWK's onboarding process within fourteen (14) calendar days of the Subscription Commencement Date.

The Subscriber notified HAWK of the Incident within twenty-four (24) hours of first becoming aware, or reasonably ought to have become aware, of the Incident.

The Subscriber engaged only a HAWK Approved Forensic Investigator, approved in writing by HAWK prior to commencement of investigation.

The root cause of the Incident has been definitively and exclusively attributed to a specific, identified, unresolved Finding within HAWK's Monitored Scope by the HAWK Approved Forensic Investigator.

The Subscriber has provided all documentation required under Part VI within the specified timeframes.

The Subscriber has signed the required non-disclosure agreement prior to any Reimbursement being applied.

The Subscriber does not have any cyber liability insurance or other insurance coverage applicable to the Incident.

All subscription fees are current with no outstanding balance at the time of Claim submission and at the time of Reimbursement.


## PART VI — CLAIM PROCESS

6.1 Initial Notification

The Subscriber must notify HAWK by sending an email to claims@securedbyhawk.com within twenty-four (24) hours of first becoming aware of the Incident. Notification by any other means does not constitute valid notification. Verbal notification, WhatsApp, SMS, or portal notification does not satisfy this requirement.

6.2 Initial Notification Content

The initial notification email must include all of the following or it will not be accepted as valid notification:

Subscriber's full legal name and company name

Subscription account number

Date and approximate time the Incident was first discovered

Brief description of the nature of the Incident

Systems and data believed to be affected

Names of all individuals with access to affected systems

Confirmation that no forensic investigation has commenced

6.3 Formal Claim Submission

Within seventy-two (72) hours of the initial notification the Subscriber must submit a formal Claim package to claims@securedbyhawk.com including:

Completed HAWK Claim Form (available upon request following initial notification)

Written incident report of minimum five hundred (500) words

All system logs, access logs, and security logs for the sixty (60) day period preceding the Incident

Screenshots of all HAWK portal activity including Finding acknowledgments and remediation records for the Compliance Period

Written confirmation of all Material Technology Changes made during the Compliance Period

Written confirmation that no cyber liability insurance claim has been or will be made for this Incident

Signed authorization for HAWK to access the Subscriber's HAWK portal records, scanning history, and remediation logs

6.4 Forensic Investigator Approval

Following receipt of a complete formal Claim package HAWK will provide a written list of HAWK Approved Forensic Investigators within ten (10) Business Days. The Subscriber must select an investigator from this list exclusively. Any forensic investigation commenced prior to HAWK's written approval is ineligible for Reimbursement.

6.5 Investigation Period

HAWK shall have a minimum of forty-five (45) Business Days from receipt of the complete formal Claim package to investigate and render a coverage decision. HAWK may extend this period by an additional thirty (30) Business Days by providing written notice to the Subscriber.

6.6 Coverage Decision

HAWK's coverage decision shall be final and binding. No appeal, arbitration, or dispute resolution process is available for coverage decisions. The Subscriber expressly waives any right to challenge HAWK's coverage decision by any means.

6.7 Non-Disclosure Agreement

Prior to any Reimbursement being applied the Subscriber must execute HAWK's standard non-disclosure agreement covering the Incident, the Claim, the investigation, the coverage decision, and the Reimbursement amount. The Subscriber agrees not to disclose any aspect of the Claim or its outcome to any third party including media, social media, review platforms, or regulatory authorities.

6.8 Reimbursement Application

Following execution of the non-disclosure agreement HAWK will apply the approved Reimbursement amount as a credit to the Subscriber's HAWK Shield subscription account within thirty (30) Business Days. Credit cannot be converted to cash or transferred.
